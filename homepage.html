How It Works

Getting started is easy. We've built a streamlined process to ensure security and speed.

1. Verify Identity

Sign up with your UIU Student ID. Our admins verify every profile to maintain a safe campus environment.

2. Connect & Bid

Post a loan request or a donation campaign. Students can bid to help with amounts they are comfortable with.

3. Build Trust

Successful repayments and active help increase your Trust Score, unlocking higher limits and faster approvals.

Built on Trust, Exclusively for the UIU Family

We believe in financial empowerment through community. Our platform is designed with security at its core to protect both lenders and borrowers.

Admin Verified IDs

Every user is cross-checked with official university records before gaining access to the network.

Transparent Bidding

Public visibility of loan terms and bidding process ensures fair treatment for everyone involved.

Direct bKash/Nagad Transfers

Funds are transferred directly student-to-student via trusted mobile banking gateways for instant access.
